Info

Journalists holding a valid press card are admitted to Copenhagen Contemporary free of charge if their visit is work-related. Please contact Line Glavind, Head of PR and Communication, prior to your visit. Journalists who do not hold a press card are very welcome to contact Ida Maj Ludvigsen before their visit.
Photographers, whether taking stills or using video, should always contact Line Glavind before their arrival and ask for permission if the photographic material is to be used for any purpose other than private. This rule applies to photos of interiors, exhibitions as well as exteriors. Special rules may also apply to particular exhibitions.
