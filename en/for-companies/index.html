Events and conferences

Make Copenhagen Contemporary, located in the heart of Refshaleøen’s budding cultural district, the venue of your next big event. The art center is about 10 minutes from Kgs. Nytorv and a mere stone’s throw from Noma, Copenhagen’s ski slope, fine dining restaurants, Reffen Copenhagen Street Food and harbour baths.

Copenhagen Contemporary’s 658 m2 event hall in B&W’s magnificent old welding hall is the ideal space for conferences, large meetings, concerts, Christmas lunches, fashion shows and marketing events. The hall’s open and raw industrial appearance allows the discerning event organiser to tailor the setup to his own needs.

The hall can take 348 people and boasts a separate entrance along with four cloakrooms. Copenhagen Contemporary collaborates with professional caterers and technicians who can help create an unforgettable day. You can also provide your own caterers if you so wish.

Bring art into your event with a guided tour

Copenhagen Contemporary offers guided tours of the current exhibitions for those who wish to combine an event with a special experience of the artworks and galleries of Copenhagen Contemporary. A guided tour lasts about 45 minutes and is designed to comply with your needs and wishes.

The minimum cost of a tour is DKK 600 with a maximum group size of 20 in connection with hire of the event hall. See more information about private tours for individuals and groups here. 

Visit the exhibitions outside of normal opening hours

If you are looking for a truly unique experience, you can explore the exhibitions of Copenhagen Contemporary after hours. A special opening of the art center can either be combined with an evening event in the event hall or organised as an independent function. Round off the evening with a glass of wine and snacks in the foyer. Contact us and find out more about cost and duration.

Children’s birthdays

Support CC

Do you want to help develop art learning activities for future generations while at the same time strengthening your company’s profile? Contact us for more information about the possibilities of becoming a sponsor or partner.

Copenhagen Contemporary’s vision is to create a vibrant meeting point where international art encounters people. Art must be accessible to everybody here, regardless of age and background.

The generous help from sponsors and partners makes it possible for Copenhagen Contemporary to realise its ambitions and carry on the work of displaying and imparting knowledge about large-scale installation art to a broad audience.

If your company would like to contribute to building the international art scene in Copenhagen, and furthering Copenhagen Contemporary’s efforts to set up art as a framework for collective experiences across generations, Copenhagen Contemporary warmly welcomes new sponsors and partners.

Prospects                

Together your company and Copenhagen Contemporary will create a meaningful format of collaboration based on your requirements. This could be anything, ranging from an exhibition sponsorship, a series of events or learning programmes to a solid partnership, stretching over several years.

The benefits of being a sponsor or partner

Copenhagen Contemporary offers a customised set of benefits to each sponsor or partner according to individual wishes. These include employee benefits, customer care and visibility.
